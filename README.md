# Bookworm - a KOReader plugin to read more at once

## Prerequisits
* A jailbroken Kindle with KOReader installed (I don't have a kobo to test this on);
* A computer and cable to plug in your device.

## Installation
1. Exit KOReader and access the native Kindle OS;
2. Connect your Kindle to your computer;
3. Access the Kindle directory and open the directory `koreader/plugins`;
4. Download the three files in this repo, (unzip if zipped), and save it in this directory in a folder with name `bookworm.plugin`;
5. Eject the Kindle device from your laptop;
6. On your Kindle, start KOReader;
7. The dialogue should appear on your screen upon startup.

## Why this plugin?

Before swapping to digital reading with my Kindle, in 2020, my analogue appetite required at least 3 entrees at once, aperitivo-style. This is easy enough to do with physical books, but digitally, the small hurdle of leaving the current view, browsing for the right file, and opening it, with the usual lags that Kindle OS boasts, make this habit harder to maintain. 
This plugin aims at making that transition easier. Upon unlocking your jailbroken device, it will prompt you to choose one among the 3 most recent unfinished (progress < .99) books in your history. The dialogue will also open when you go to the directory overview. It can also be manually triggered.
Hopefully this helps you, too!
